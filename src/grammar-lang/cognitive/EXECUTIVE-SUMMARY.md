### Chomsky Hierarchy (5 Layers)
```
PHONEMES (tone, rhythm, pitch)
    ↓
MORPHEMES (keywords, negations)
    ↓
SYNTAX (grammatical patterns)
    ↓
SEMANTICS (meaning analysis)
    ↓
PRAGMATICS (intent detection)
```
